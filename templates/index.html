GHG Predictor

The Green House Gas (GHG) Predictor Based on Gross Domestic Product (GDP) Per Capita

The GHG predictor takes a numeric input of the per capita GDP and outputs a predicted level of GHG produced based on that input.

This calculation is accomplished by compairing per capita GDP by nation to the GHG per capita by nation.

Linear regression is used to train a machine learning algorithm to make predictions.
